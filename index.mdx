## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a wallet">
    Create a [MetaMask](https://metamask.io) wallet and fund your account. This wallet will be used for on-chain trading on supported exchanges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get exchange API keys">
    Obtain API keys from each prediction market exchange you want to trade on:
    - [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com)
    - [Kalshi](https://kalshi.com)
    - [Limitless](https://limitless.exchange)
    - [Opinion](https://opinion.trade)
    - [Predict.fun](https://predict.fun)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Log in to your account and go to the **Settings** page. Fill in your exchange credentials and press **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your Parsec API key">
    Reveal your Parsec API key at the top of the Settings page and integrate it into your trading system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Your Parsec API key will only work if your prediction market exchange credentials were entered correctly.
</Note>
